# Governance

The Strategic Opportunities (Growth & Income) Fund (“the Fund”) is an Australian registered (managed) fund (scheme) ARSN 668 357 837.

The Fund is governed by its Responsible Entity pursuant to its Constitution.

The Fund’s Responsible Entity (the entity that holds an Australian Financial Services Licence and acts as a responsible entity for the Fund) is Plantation Capital Limited (“PCL”) (AFSL No 339481).

PCL is governed by its Board of Directors, which presently consists of three directors (see below). The Board provides executive oversight and concentrates on vision, strategy, compliance and portfolio / financial performance. The Board usually meets monthly online or in person in Melbourne.
